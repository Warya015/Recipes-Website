Somali Coussine

For our first recipes, we'll be diving into the Somali Coussine. The Somali people are known for their good seasoning and variety of meat that goes along with their main dishes. It varies alot from pastas with tuna that are prepared within 45 minutes, to larger meals with alot of meat that takes up to 2 hours. Let's start with a few simple ones and spice it up along the way.

Let's kick it off with some side dishes to get your stomach going.

  1. Goat Stew

    2. Goat meat is staple in somali cooking and most widely preferred, this goat stew makes an excellent side dish to be served

  2. Kebabo Beef

    3. Beef Kebab made with finely minced meat with fresh cilantro to make it more spicy add chili for a little heat if you like.They are pan fried on skillet a great side dish they could be snack for anytime of the day.

Here are some filling main dishes!

  1. Rice with LAMB

    2. Lamb surbiyaan, this is one pot dish both delicious with lamb or goat, toped with raisins, caramelized onions and saffron treads. Serve this dish with vegetables and salad, it is usually served on occasions such as Eid. Use always the best quility basmati rice that you can find for flavour and the texture for a fluffy rice.

  2. Vermicelli Noodles

    3. Rice cooked with Vermicelli this combination go together so well with. lamb Stew or as a side dish. Vermicelli can be found in Indian grocery stores.
