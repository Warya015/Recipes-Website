Lamb with Rice

Main dishes

Ingredients

Cramelized onion and raisins

  1. 1/4 cup oil
  2. 1 onion sliced
  3. 1/2 cup raisins

Saffron

  1. 1/4 cup oil
  2. 1 teaspoon saffron heads

Boiling the rice

  1. 3 cups basmati rice
  2. 5-6 cups of water

The lamb sauce

  1. 2 pounds lamb, medium cut
  2. 1 small onion, chopped
  3. 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  4. 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  5. 1-2 tablespoons coriander/cilantro leaves
  6. 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  7. 2 teaspoons cumin powder
  8. 1-2 teaspoons paprika or a cayenne pepper
  9. 2-4 tablespoons oil (use the left over from the caramelized onions)
  10. 5 cardamom pods cracked
  11. 2 cinnamon sticks
  12. 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Instructions

Caramelized onion and raisins

  1. Heat the oil over medium heat, add the onion and fry until nice and brown.
  2. Add the raisins and cook until they puff up.
  3. Drain on a kitchen paper.
  4. Keep this a side we will, add this mixture over rice.
  5. Fry them in a large skillet with little oil, on a medium heat and fry them both sides.
  6. Enjoy!

Soak the Saffron

  1. In a small bowl add the saffron pour hot water into the bowl.
  2. let is soak for 4- 20 minutes

Boiling the Rice

  1. Start boiling the water in a sauce pan.
  2. When boiled, add the rice and salt to sate.
  3. Cook about 4 minutes
  4. Depending on your heat the rice should be cooked half way, test it.
  5. Drain the rice in a colander and let it cool until needed

Cooking the lamb.

  1. In a large pot fry the lamb with the oil until brown both sides.
  2. Add onions saute until brown.
  3. Add cardamom, cinnamon sticks, lemon juice, then add gardlic & add tomatoes, spices and cilantro.
  4. Mix this together until combined then cook for 3 minutes until fragrant, add the 1/2 cup of water
  5. Let it simmer while covered on a medium heat until boil and then lower the heat for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Now we need the boiled rice add it to the lamb sauce, scrape the top evenly.
  7. Add the caramelized onion and raisins.
  8. Add the saffron water, cover the pot and cook on a medium heat until boil, then reduced the heat on a simmer.
  9. The bottom of the pan will be dry and there will be no water left, make sure not to burn the bottom.
  10. The rice should now be fluffy. Enjoy!
