Vermicelli Noodles & Rice

Main dishes

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups Basmati rice
  2. 3 cups of water
  3. 1 cup of Vermicelli
  4. 1 small onion, chopped
  5. 4 tablespoons oil
  6. salt

Instructions

  1. Was the rice cand let it soak for 30 minutes
  2. Break the vermicelli noodles into small pieces. Fry them with 2 tablespoons of oil on a skillet until golden color, set the pan aside
  3. In a sauce pan add 2 tablespoons oil and fry the onions for 4 minutes.
  4. Add the drained rice and 3 cups water season with salt, bring to a boil turn down the heat and leave it to simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the vermicelli to the rice and cover.
  6. Mix well with the rice and cover the pan and simmer on a low heat until the rice has soaked all the water and both the rice and vermicelli are tender.
  7. Enjoy!
