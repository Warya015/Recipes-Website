Coconut Squares

Desserts

Ingredients

  1. 395 can sweetened condensed milk
  2. 2 cups icing sugar.
  3. 3 1/2 cups desiccated coconut
  4. Few drops of red and green food coloring.

Instructions

  1. Grease and line your try with baking paper.
  2. In a bowl add the condensed milk, cocunut and stir until combined.
  3. Divide the mixture equally into three bowls and add food coloring into two bowls, few drops at a time until it reaches the right color mix.
  4. Place the red mixture into the base and use a spoon to flatten it out. Follow up by placing the white mixture and eventually the green mixture.
  5. Refrigerate for 1 hour, once it has set, cut it into squares.
  6. Enjoy!
