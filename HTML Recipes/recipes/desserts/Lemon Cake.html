Lemon Yogurt Cake

Desserts

Ingredients

  1. 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour.
  2. 2 teaspoons baking powder.
  3. 4 Eggs.
  4. 1 Cup of sugar.
  5. 2/3 Cup of yogurt.
  6. 2/3 cup yogurt.
  7. 4 Table spoons lemon juice.
  8. 1 Tablespoon grated lemon grind.
  9. 2/3 Cups of oil.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven 350f - 180c
  2. grease an loaf pan or 8 inch/20cm cake pan.
  3. In a mixing bowl add flour, baking powder and sugar and mix to combine.
  4. Pour the wet and the dry ingredient together and mix to combine.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake in the preheated oven for 40-50 minutes or until risen and golden brown.
  7. Cool and serve, Enjoy!
